Process Model Information


This is a BioProcess model. It was designed with the goal of having few inputs while producing quality results. It is useful in predicting yields and productivites given the contraints of maximum oxygen levels and volume utilization. This model is simple. It allow users to quickly compare data and observations. The model is self-limiting in relation to temperature and oxygen, and this gives the model its simplicity and uniqueness.

Features

  • Change any input and click desired button to graph
  • Click the box-labels in the legend to toggle each plot
  • Adjust xOUR min for process (related to O2/product and biomass etc.)
  • Key outputs are listed below inputs

In The Near Future!
